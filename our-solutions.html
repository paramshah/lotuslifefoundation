Explore Our Solutions

With every project we've worked on, we have met different people, worked with unique partners, and encountered challenging obstacles. We have developed compassionate solutions that engage the entire community in our work, providing a holistic approach to service.

Community-Based Rehabilitation

Treatment programs for people with disabilities that facilitate social integration, equalization of opportunities and rehabilitation efforts.

  •
Grassroots Health Worker Training Programs

Formally training ambitious individuals in rural communities to learn skills ranging from physical therapy to medical device repair.

  •
Assistive Devices

Based on the person's condition, assistive devices such as walkers, orthotics, or prosthetic devices are needed to supplement the treatment.

Surgical Intervention

Organizing medical camps where trained orthopedic surgeons assess and operate on people with severe medical needs.

Community Education

Advocacy efforts to provide knowledge and guidance to families and communities on how to care for a person with disabilities.
